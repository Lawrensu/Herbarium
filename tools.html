Tools Required

1. Fresh Leaf Specimen

2. Absorbent Paper (e.g., Blotting Paper)

3. Hard, Flat Surfaces (e.g., Wooden Boards)

4. Weights or Straps

5. Glue

6. Herbarium Sheet (Sturdy White Paper)

7. Pen for Labeling

8. Herbarium Cabinet or Folder for Storage
